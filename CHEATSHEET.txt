# Cheatsheet
ToLaserBlade-BTA v1.0.0

## Items
### Laser Blade
- Default ID: 24530 (This can be changed in config/tolaserblade.cfg, `starting_item_id + 0`)
- Tool type: sword
- Durability: infinite
- Basic attack damage: 12
- Crafting on Crafting Table: G = Glowstone Dust; S = Steel Ingot, D = Diamond; L = Lamp; R = Redstone
  - GSD;
  - SLS;
  - RSG;
- Laser blades have 16 different colors, which are determined by the color of the lamp in the crafting material
